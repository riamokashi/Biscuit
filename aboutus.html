About The Team



The Biscuit Team consisted of first time coders Ria Mokashi, Marcux Brown, and Robert Bush. In the Computer Science Summer Institute at Google Detroit, all three students put together their individual coding skills to build an innnovative web application. The team is excited to explore future oppportunities.

Before I started CSSI, I had no coding experience at all. By the end of the program, I was able to work with my amazing teammates to build this entire web application in only 4 days, along with the help of our instructors. I had an amazing experience at Google Detroit in CSSI, and I am so excited to see what else the future will bring me in my tech career!
